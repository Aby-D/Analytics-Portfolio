This dashboard provides a baseline view of the bank’s customer demographics across the UK regions.
England shows a strong concentration of white‑collar workers (around 70%), indicating a customer base with stable income and professional backgrounds.
Scotland is predominantly represented by male customers in their late 40s to early 50s, with a lower proportion of white‑collar workers, suggesting different economic and occupational patterns.
Wales displays an above‑average share of customers with mid‑sized account balances, indicating a financially stable but moderately affluent customer segment.
Northern Ireland is largely represented by younger female customers, highlighting a distinct demographic profile compared to other regions.
These regional differences help the bank tailor marketing, product offerings, and customer engagement strategies based on demographic and financial behavior patterns.
