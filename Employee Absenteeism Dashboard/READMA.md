Employee Absenteeism Dashboard — Workforce Trends & Insights
This dashboard analyzes employee absenteeism using the MFGEmployees4 dataset, providing clear visibility into workforce attendance patterns, productivity risks, and HR‑related KPIs. It helps organizations identify high‑risk groups, understand absence trends, and make data‑driven decisions to improve workforce efficiency.

Business Problem Solved
Absenteeism directly impacts productivity, labor costs, and operational efficiency.
This dashboard enables HR and management teams to:
Detect attendance issues early
Understand root causes and patterns
Improve workforce planning
Support data‑driven HR decisions


Tools & Skills Used
Power BI (data modeling, DAX, interactive visuals)
Data Cleaning & Transformation
HR Analytics
KPI Development & Trend Analysis
