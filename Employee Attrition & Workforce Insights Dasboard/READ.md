# Employee Attrition & Workforce Insights Dashboard

This Power BI dashboard analyzes employee attrition patterns using the IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition & Performance dataset (1,470 records, 35 HR variables). The report highlights key workforce trends, attrition drivers, and risk indicators to support HR decision-making.

## Key Features
- Overall Attrition Rate and Workforce KPIs
- **Voluntary vs. Involuntary Attrition analysis** with custom logic
- Attrition breakdown by Department, Job Role, Education, and Age Group
- Salary, Overtime, and Job Satisfaction impact on attrition
- Interactive slicers for Department, Job Role, Education, and Gender
- Visual insights into workforce stability and high‑risk employee segments

## Voluntary & Involuntary Attrition Logic
A custom classification was created to separate attrition into:
- **Voluntary Attrition** – employees who left by choice (e.g., resignation)
- **Involuntary Attrition** – employees who left due to organizational decisions

This helps HR teams understand whether turnover is driven by employee dissatisfaction or structural factors.

## Purpose
This dashboard helps HR teams identify:
- Which employee groups have the highest attrition
- What factors contribute most to turnover
- Whether attrition is voluntary or involuntary
- Where retention strategies should be focused
