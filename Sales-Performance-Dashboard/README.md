# Sales Performance Dashboard — Revenue, Profit & Growth Insights

This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of sales performance across revenue, profit, customer behavior, and product trends. It includes advanced interactive features such as customer order drill-through, segment-level tooltips, and a detailed profit analysis table to support deeper business insights.

## Key Features
- Total Revenue, Total Profit, Profit Margin %, and YoY Growth KPIs
- Sales performance by Region, Product Category, and Customer Segment
- Monthly and Quarterly Sales Trends
- Top/Bottom Performing Products and Regions
- **Customer Orders Drill-Through** for transaction-level insights
- **Segment Tooltip Pages** for contextual segment-level summaries
- **Profit Analysis Table** showing revenue, cost, profit, and margin by product/region
- Interactive slicers for Region, Product Category, and Time Period
- Visual insights into sales distribution, profitability, and growth patterns

## Advanced Interactions
### Customer Orders Drill-Through
A dedicated drill-through page allows users to:
- View detailed order-level data for any product or region
- Analyze customer purchase patterns
- Understand revenue contribution at a granular level

### Segment Tooltip Pages
Custom tooltip pages provide:
- Segment-level summaries
- Quick insights without navigating away from the main page
- A smoother, more intuitive user experience

### Profit Analysis Table
A detailed table that includes:
- Revenue, Cost, and Profit
- Profit Margin %
- Product-level and region-level profitability insights

## Purpose
This dashboard enables sales and leadership teams to:
- Monitor overall sales health and profitability
- Identify growth opportunities and declining segments
- Compare performance across regions and product lines
- Explore customer-level insights through drill-through functionality
- Evaluate profitability using detailed profit analysis
- Support data-driven decision-making for sales strategy
